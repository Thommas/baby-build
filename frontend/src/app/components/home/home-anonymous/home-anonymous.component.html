Child education gamification platform

Have fun taking care of your child

Path of child is a gamification platform to help parents plan the long term education of their child.
Organize digital contents that the child will slowly get access to.
Each parent can tailor the platform to match their vision of education.

Core platform values

Technology control

Parents are putting their children in danger by letting them passively consume digital contents.

Dopamine control

Cultivate depth and curiosity instead of short interaction and waste of time.

Community powered

This is a place where parents care. The platform will help share popular education tips.

Collaboration tool

Other family members can also contribute.

Main features

  • Limited time investment, it takes a few minutes every day to use the platform.
  • We want to find a nice balance between acting like big brother and real security concerns.
  • Make access to technology a reward for the child.
  • Community tools.
  • Promote gender equity.
  • Allow specific culture, religion, belief, family traditions.
